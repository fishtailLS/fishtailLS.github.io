About

Fishtail Language Services provides highest quality service to clients with English-Nepali (नेपाली) language needs. Based in California, we have decade long experience of serving many sectors ranging from technology, banking, legal, healthcare to government affairs. And we're always seeking new opportunities.

Academically trained and professionally experienced in both the United States and Nepal. Fluent in language, culture and working ways of both countries. ACTFL certified language analysts. Also Certified as Oral Proficiency Interview (OPI) testers.

Why Fishtail?
We are named after the gorgeous mountain peak in central Nepal - Mt. Machhapuchhre (माछापुच्छ्रे) or Fishtail (elev. 22,943 ft, 6,993 m), seen here at the center of vista above.

Translation

Professional translation of written documents in English to/from Nepali language.

Interpretation

Phone based, online, video and on-site interpretation between spoken English and Nepali languages.

Localization

Planning and implementation of language and culture based localization projects for software, website, apps.

Cultural Counsel

Facilitate communication for best outcome in overlapping context of English and Nepali speakers.
